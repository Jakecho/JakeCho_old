A Psychometricain and Data Scientist

Philosophy:

"An item does not have its intrinsic properties in dimensionality, scale, difficulty, discrimination, guessing, etc. Such properties are rather established upon both substantive theories and measurement instruments that are theoretically conceptualized, and that are purposefully designed, operationalized, and evaluated against a target population through the process of the item and scale development. Thus, the beauty of psychometrics work is to allow one to make a valid evidence-based inference between the score which is the summary of item responses, and its target group in terms of the construct(s) of interest."
